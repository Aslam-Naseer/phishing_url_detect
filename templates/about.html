About Us

Phishsnooper

Phishsnooper is an innovative web application designed to empower users with enhanced online security. Leveraging cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, Phishsnooper swiftly identifies potentially harmful URLs, safeguarding users from phishing attempts, malware, and fake websites. With its intuitive interface, users can easily analyze URLs for safety, gaining confidence while browsing the internet. Phishsnooper's commitment to staying ahead of emerging cyber threats ensures that users can navigate the digital landscape with peace of mind, knowing that their online safety is prioritized.

How Does Phishsnooper Work?

Phishsnooper uses machine learing models trained on a dataset on 11000+ URLs to predict whether a given URL is suspicious or not. Packed with several features :

  • Estimate of URL safety
  • Overview of sensitive features
  • User awareness and experience oriented interface
